How It Works

Step 1: Account Registration:

Start by registering on our website. Fill in your details to create a new account, or log in to an existing one. Registering gives you access to our warehouses in Dubai, the USA, and Canada.

Getting a personal address in the UAE:

After registration, you will receive a unique delivery address in the UAE, which can be used for your shopping.

Step 2: Shop at any UAE store

Enter your unique UAE address at checkout:

When placing an order, use the unique UAE address provided by us. The store will ship your order to this address, and you can then add it to your waiting list in your personal account on our website.

Step 3: Arrival of the package:

Once your order is received at the warehouse, you will be notified via your registered email address.

Inspection of the package:

Our staff will verify the package to ensure the goods are present and match the details in the customs declaration.

Step 4: Shipping:

Once the shipping invoice is paid, the package will be dispatched to the destination address.

Tracking:

Each package is assigned a unique tracking number to track its progress at every stage of transit, until it is delivered to the recipient.

Step 5: Delivery:

The package will be delivered to the specified destination address by EMS courier, directly to your door. Delivery times vary based on the destination country and recipient's address.
