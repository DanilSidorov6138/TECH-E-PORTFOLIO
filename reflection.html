This was a research paper done for the class HDFS 497, HUMAN DEVELOPMENT AND FAMILY STUDIES 497. The assignment was to pick a piece of media and analyze the different themes as it relates to the course. For my research paper I chose to write about Health Disparities in America and how it compares to other countries. The paper is 13 pages long, about 4000 words, and I achieved a final grade of 96%. This piece of writing required in-depth research, combining multiple pieces of complex research, and then finally presenting it in a structured and persuasive manner. This media analysis paper accurately reflects my ability to communicate complex information succinctly as well as my ability to construct logical arguments based on evidence. I want potential employers to see this paper and see my dedication to crafting engaging and persuasive arguments as well as my commitment to producing quality work.
